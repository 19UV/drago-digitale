Usage

This app will generate a word, which you can plug into Wordle to get the response. The response can be given to this app, and it will try and generate the next best (or good) word.

By just typing in a letter (A, B, or C), the letter will show up on the grid. A is for green, B for yellow, and C for gray. You can use backspace, and enter once you've entered in all 5 letters. Be sure that you enter them in correctly, or it won't give a good answer.

Note: Entering in AAAAA will clear the board
